The Journey

Born with rhythm in her veins, DJ Ngaks discovered her passion for electronic music at the age of 16. Starting in underground clubs, she quickly rose through the ranks with her unique blend of progressive house, techno, and melodic bass lines.

Her innovative approach to mixing and her ability to read the crowd has made her one of the most sought-after DJs in the international scene. From intimate club settings to massive festival stages, Ngaks creates an atmosphere that transcends the ordinary.

Beyond the decks, she's a producer, mentor, and advocate for women in electronic music, constantly pushing boundaries and inspiring the next generation of artists.
